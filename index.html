🛡️ Client Security Standards

Layered Defense Strategy

Mandatory security protocols enforced for all managed infrastructure.

1. Passphrase Enforcement:

Minimum 14-character passphrases required. We strictly mandate the use of Password Managers (Bitwarden/1Password) to eliminate credential theft.

2. Mandatory MFA:

Hardware or App-based Multi-Factor Authentication required for all entry points, mitigating 99% of bulk automated attacks.

3. Zero-Trust Perimeter:

Login screens (/admin) are hidden behind an Identity-Aware Proxy. Access is invisible to the public and restricted by verified identity.

4. Geofencing & Encryption:

Enforcement of TLS 1.3/HSTS and country-level access control to protect customer data and build international trust.
