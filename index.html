🛡️ Client Security & Authentication Guide

Layered Defense Strategy
1. The "Golden Rule" of Passwords:

We enforce a minimum of 14 characters using complex passphrases. We strictly mandate Password Managers to eliminate plain-text vulnerabilities.

2. Mandatory Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA):

MFA is required on all entry points. We prioritize Authenticator Apps and Hardware Keys to mitigate 99% of bulk automated attacks.

3. Zero-Trust Perimeter (Cloudflare IAP):

Login screens are hidden behind an Identity-Aware Proxy. The administration interface remains invisible to the public, requiring verified identity for access.

4. Continuous Monitoring & Response:

Enforcement of TLS 1.3/HSTS and strategic geofencing. We monitor login patterns to identify and block brute-force attempts in real-time.
